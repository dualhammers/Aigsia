# White Bean & Green Stew

## Ingridients
- 1/2 onion
- Garlic
- Italian seasoning
- Boullion (1-2 tsp)
- Tomato paste 6oz
- 1 cup white beans
- 1 cup dry pasta
- 1 brussel sprout, chopped
- 1 cup greens (kale or spinach or whatever)

## Directions
- Saute onion
- Add tomato paste, water, and seasoning
- stir
- Add beans and pasta
- boil for 5 minutes
- Add greens
- Boil another 5 minutes
